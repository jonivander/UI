PotLucky!

An app designed to take the headache out of potluck planning. Lucky you!

Gather your friends and family without the hassle and the potluck table full of desserts and nothing else. With just a few taps, you can take care of everything you need to get everyone in the same place for a great time.

GET STARTED

Create Your Event

Easily plan your event, including date, time, location, guest list, and dish ideas.

Share Your Event

Share your event details with your guests, where they can RSVP and sign up for the items they want to bring or suggest their own.

Gather 'Round

No headache involved! When the day comes, just relax and enjoy your gathering knowing all the details are taken care of. Lucky you!
